The case for connected campus safety

A campus is a community — it deserves a safety net that reaches every corner.

From the late-night walk back from the library to the morning learner transport run, the moments that matter most happen away from the security desk. Fixed guards and CCTV simply can't be everywhere at once.

Mjne World closes that gap with a layer of wearable devices and live monitoring — so a student in distress, a lone staff member or a missing asset becomes an instant, located alert rather than a delayed phone call. We design the right mix for your campus and deploy it.

Where it matters

Built for the realities of campus life.

Four everyday risks, one safety system that responds in seconds — wherever students and staff happen to be.

Night-time student safety

Walking to residences, parking areas or the library after dark becomes far less daunting. A discreet, long-press SOS dials up to three preset responders in a loop while sharing live GPS — so help knows exactly where to go.

Learner & student transport

Follow every journey from the gate to the drop-off on a live map. Geo-fenced safe zones flag when a vehicle or learner leaves an expected route, giving transport officers and parents real, route-level peace of mind.

Lone-worker & staff protection

Cleaners, lab technicians, after-hours lecturers and security staff often work alone across large grounds. A wearable panic device gives every lone worker an immediate, located lifeline back to your control room.

Asset & equipment security

Laptops, lab equipment, generators and fleet vehicles represent serious investment. Compact GPS trackers and safe-zone alerts help your team monitor high-value assets and respond the moment something moves out of bounds.
