How a connected CPF works

From one press to a coordinated response.

Every member becomes a node in one live network — equipped, visible and backed by the people around them.

Equip every member

Hand out panic wearables and discreet SOS buttons across the forum. One long press fires an emergency and dials three preset numbers in a loop until someone answers.

Alerts hit a shared live dashboard

Every alert lands on one central group dashboard with the member's exact location — live GPS to within 10 metres over 4G, on any phone or screen.

Coordinate the nearest responder

The forum sees who is closest and sends them straight to the spot — no guessing at addresses, no waiting for a guard on the far side of the suburb.

Review patterns over time

Incidents and locations build a clear picture, so your CPF can patrol smarter, brief SAPS with facts and protect the streets that need it most.
