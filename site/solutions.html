Mjne Tech · IoT & Security

SOS panic and live GPS for people, livestock and assets.

One platform for both security and health emergencies — instant SOS panic paired with real-time GPS tracking, so you carry instant peace of mind wherever you are. Whether it's a person, a herd or a high-value asset, help and location are always within reach.

We deploy cutting-edge IoT infrastructure designed to eliminate blind spots and extend your panic radius, so you feel secure everywhere you go. It runs from discreet wearable smartwatches with instant SOS panic buttons that protect vulnerable family members, through to rugged GPS tracking hardware that secures livestock and corporate assets — putting control back in your hands. Multi-source GPS, WiFi and LBS positioning pinpoints within 10 metres over 4G, while one press of the SOS button dials three preset numbers in a loop until someone answers.

  • One-press SOSA long press dials three preset numbers in a loop and shares a live location.
  • Meter-level GPSGPS, WiFi and LBS combine for stable, sub-10 m positioning on 4G.
  • Livestock & asset securityAutomated tracking for herds, fleets and high-value equipment.

Mjne Green Mobility · Renewable Energy

Clean transport and tailored solar that move you further on less.

Green Mobility is where innovative transport meets customisable renewable energy. We sell commercial solar golf carts, run fleet rentals, and craft bespoke eco-friendly vehicle conversions — practical clean mobility for estates, lodges, campuses and farms across the country.

Beyond the wheels, we design tailored industrial solar installations that decarbonise corporate and agricultural operations — trimming diesel bills, smoothing load-shedding and turning your roof into a quietly working asset. One partner, from the first site visit to the last panel commissioned.

Mjne Agrotech · Smart Agriculture

Climate-smart farming that feeds communities and closes the loop.

Agrotech is our answer to food insecurity and a changing climate. We manufacture premium vertical hydroponic towers right here in South Africa, built from robust, weather-resistant materials engineered to thrive in tough rural and township conditions — compact, productive and built to last.

Each deployment carries our Rural Hydroponic, Food Security & Circular Economy System: we teach climate-resilient farming and recycle agroprocessing waste into high-value organic animal feed and nutrient-rich compost. Less waste, more harvest, and dignified livelihoods that stay in the community.

Mjne Auto Parts · Automotive Supply

Premium parts that keep South Africa moving.

Mjne Auto Parts is the supply backbone of the ecosystem — premium supply and sales of high-quality automotive parts and spares. From the commercial fleet to the farm bakkie to the family car, we keep essential vehicles moving safely and efficiently.

Downtime is expensive, and in many communities a stranded vehicle stalls an entire livelihood. So we've built a dependable supply chain of genuine, hard-wearing components — sourced well, priced fairly and delivered when you need them, so the wheels never stop turning longer than they have to.

Enterprise Development · Mentorship & Community

Bridging grassroots talent with real economic systems.

Technology only changes lives when people can build with it. Through ITSCIE — our Ideation To Startup Centre of Innovation and Entrepreneurship — and the Her Opportunity Accelerator, we deliver structured business mentorship, digital learning and practical enterprise development to the founders who need it most.

Our programmes are tailored to uplift youth and women entrepreneurs in rural markets — meeting them where they are, equipping them with skills and networks, and connecting their ambition to commercial economic systems. It's how an idea in a township becomes an enterprise that employs a community.
