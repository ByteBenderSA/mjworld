Mjne Agrotech · Smart Agriculture

Climate-smart farming that feeds communities and closes the loop.

Agrotech is our answer to food insecurity and a changing climate. We manufacture premium vertical hydroponic towers right here in South Africa, purpose-built for African climate conditions — robust, weather-resistant materials engineered to thrive in tough rural and township conditions, compact, productive and built to last.

We're just as focused on empowering smallholder farmers as we are on serving commercial growers — because food security starts on the smallest plots. Each deployment carries our Rural Hydroponic, Food Security & Circular Economy System: we teach climate-resilient farming and recycle agroprocessing waste into high-value organic animal feed and nutrient-rich compost. Less waste, more harvest, and dignified livelihoods that stay in the community.
