Built for the school run

Everything a parent needs.

Live route, gate to door

Watch the journey move in real time on the map, and scroll back through the full route history to see exactly where the transport went and when it arrived.

Safe-zone & geo-fence alerts

Draw a safe zone around the school and around home. You're notified the moment your child arrives or leaves — no more refreshing the app or waiting for a call.

One-press SOS

Why parents choose it

Accurate to the metre, all the way home.

By combining GPS, WiFi and LBS signals, the tracker fixes a meter-level position with stronger anti-interference and steadier tracking — so the blue dot on your map is genuinely where your child is, not a guess.

  • Under 10 m accuracyMulti-source positioning pinpoints the journey precisely, on busy roads and quiet streets alike.
  • Two-way reassuranceReach your child or the SOS contacts in a moment — a quick check-in is never more than a press away.
  • Family dashboardBoth parents follow the same live location, route history and alerts from one simple app.
Made for real school days

Rugged enough for everyday.

Children's bags get dropped, rained on and stuffed under seats. This tracker is splash and rain resistant and built to shrug off the knocks of an ordinary school day, so it keeps working when life gets messy.

  • Splash & rain resistantA sudden downpour or a spilled water bottle won't stop it tracking the way home.
  • Knock-tolerant buildA compact, solid body handles being tossed in a backpack or rattling around a vehicle.
  • Multi-day batteryA long-lasting charge means fewer top-ups — set it once and it's ready for the week ahead.
