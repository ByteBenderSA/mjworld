How the network works

From a single press to immediate help.

Every Mjne Tech device runs on a connected 4G ecosystem — combining multi-source positioning, instant alerts and a live dashboard your whole family or team can see.

  • PressA long-press on the SOS button triggers an emergency in seconds.
  • ConnectThe device dials up to three preset numbers in a loop and shares live GPS.
  • RespondResponders see exact location on a live map and act with confidence.
