About Mjne World

Building tech-enabled impact, from the ground up.

Mjne World (Pty) Ltd is an IoT service-based, innovation-driven enterprise. We turn traditional operations into high-impact, tech-enabled solutions — pioneering climate-smart agriculture, clean energy, advanced security and connected retail for South Africa's rural areas and townships.

Our story

Innovation that starts where it's needed most.

We were built on a simple conviction: the communities too often left on the margins of progress are exactly where the next wave of innovation belongs. So we plant it there first.

Across rural areas and townships, that conviction shows up as working systems, not promises — safer streets, cleaner energy, food grown closer to home, and enterprises that can finally scale. From a single SOS device on a grandmother's wrist to a solar fleet on an estate, every project answers the same question: how do we bridge grassroots talent with the real economy? That is the work, and it is proudly South African.

What drives us

Mission, vision and the way we work.

Three commitments hold everything we build together — and keep us honest about who it is for.

Mission

Development that lasts

To deliver sustainable development and economic transformation in the places that need it most — replacing traditional operations with high-impact, tech-enabled solutions that communities can own and grow.

Vision

Climate-smart, connected communities

A South Africa where rural areas and townships run on clean energy, climate-resilient food systems and connected security — communities that are safer, greener and economically self-reliant.

Approach

Transition, don't replace people

We move traditional operations into the tech-enabled era — designing, deploying and supporting complete ecosystems end to end, so technology lifts the people already doing the work rather than leaving them behind.

Leadership

Led by a builder of ecosystems.

Mjne World is founded and led by Managing Director Maryjane Mphahlele — a seasoned social entrepreneur and ecosystem builder who has spent her career connecting overlooked talent to opportunity.

Under her direction, every division is held to the same standard — does it create lasting work, safety and dignity on the ground? It's leadership that keeps the company close to the communities it serves.
